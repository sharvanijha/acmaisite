    events

    check out the zero week mixer! rsvp here.

    Looking to meet some super cool people from our award-winning Bollywood dance team Nashaa? Want to try Bhangra but scared of hell week (7 hour practices a night!)? Want to just chill and check out UCLA's South Asian clubs? Come to our Zero Week Mixer, where you can meet representatives from all of UCLA's South Asian organizations! From dance to singing to something else I'm probably blanking on a good word for, there's bound to be something you're interested in at this event. And if not, we've got some bomb South Asian snacks. Come thru!

    apply to our internship! access the app here.

    The Indus internship is a yearlong program which gives you the opportunity to take on responsibility within the club - you can help out with our events throughout the year as well as engage with the UCLA community through the culminating event of the program: the internship campaign. Apply now! Applications are due by Month Day at 11:59 pm.

    come to our fall quarter general meeting!

    Want to learn what Indus is all about, meet some fellow South Asians, and enjoy the only authentic South Asian snacks you can probably find on campus? Stop by Indus' fall quarter general meeting and learn what Indus has in store for you this year! Meet our new board (and maybe increase your chances of becoming an intern!) and get pumped up for a great new school year. See you there!
